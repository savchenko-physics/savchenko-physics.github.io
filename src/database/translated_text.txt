We use the formula obtained in <a href="../5.3.1">5.3.1</a> for the mean free path
$$ \lambda = \frac{1}{\pi d^2n}\quad(1) $$
The concentration of particles can be expressed through the equation of state of an ideal gas
$$ p=nkT_0\Rightarrow n=\frac{p}{kT_0}\quad(2) $$
Substituting $(2)$ into $(1)$, we get
$$ \boxed{\lambda = \frac{kT_0}{\sqrt{2}\pi d^2p}\approx60\text{ нм}}\quad(3) $$
